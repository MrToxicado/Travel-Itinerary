Exclusive Excursion of the City

Events And Festivals

Attending various events is one of the best ways to discover local culture and traditions. Still, most often travellers choose sightseeing tours or eating out over the festivals, fashion shows, etc. Actually, figuring out what to do and where to go in such a large city like Milan is quite a challenge.

Formula I Grand Prix

While some of us remember dancing in September, auto racing fans should be checking the dates for the Italian Formula 1 Grand Prix. It takes place in Autodromo Nazionale Monza, located 30 km north of Milan.






Christmas and New Year's Eve

Winter is a magical season in Milan. The upcoming Christmas and New Year’s Eve bring pure joy and excitement to daily life. Shops, restaurants, and streets are beautifully decorated with Christmas colours and the immense dome of the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II is covered in Christmas lightbulbs!







Teatre alla Scala

While the city looks fascinating and festive, on December 7th the famous Teatro alla Scala celebrates the opening of the season. The unique night at the opera theatre is popular among the elite guests and celebrities, so the tickets are pricey and limited. However, cinemas and cultural venues broadcast the performances of the opening night live.





International Classic Music

Milan Summer Festival brings all the music lovers together. The lineup is different every year – from rock star performances to Latino music beats – and the organizing staff tries to assure that there’s something for everyone’s taste. .

Lifestyle and Fun

Naviglio

For the young people who frequent the canal-side cafés and music clubs,Naviglio is one of the top things to do in Milan at night. It's the most active in the evening, for the restaurants and frequent festivals held here.

La Scala Opera

This opera house is one of the finest in the world and is renowned for its acoustic qualities and opulence.The grandeur of the seating and stage is fantastic and individual boxes frame the stage and the whole place is full of red velvet drapes and gold furnishings.

Brera District

A distinct Bohemian atmosphere prevails here and the district is full of art academies, galleries and well-to-do restaurants and bars.The vibe here is fantastic and you can enjoy a fine meal,or simply walk the many cobbleds streets and admire the architecture.
