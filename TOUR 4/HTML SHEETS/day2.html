Events And Festivals

Steeped in history, style and culture, the City of Light has more than enough to draw visitors all year around – and that’s before we even get started on the food and wine. But there are certain times of year when a trip to Paris can be extra-special.

Fall Semester

If you’ll be coming to Paris in the fall, you’re in for a treat.The Festival d’Automne à Paris, this multidisciplinary contemporary art festival.It takes place in 47 different venues throughout Paris.It brings a wide variety of performances and exhibitions including visual arts, theatre, dance, music, and film.

All-night-long party Nuit Blanche landmarks the Eiffel Tower, Louvre and Pompidou stay open super-late, while artists from around the world set up temporary exhibits and installations across the city.






Spring Semester

Spring in Paris evokes quaint streets bursting with cherry blossoms, parks erupting in lush shades, and Parisians and visitors alike shedding their winter coats to enjoy the milder weather. The City of Light does not disappoint during this season,and the paris carnival compltes the season with influences from endless cultural and religious groups.







Summer Semester

During the Summers,the city gives way to two of the most unique events of the Paris.The Bastille Day and the Paris Jazz Festival. The Paris Jazz Festival has hosted some of the most revered names in jazz from around the world, making it a unique opportunity to experience live performances of some of these legendary artists.As a visitor to France, you won’t want to miss this special national holiday.July 14 France marks the storming of the Bastille prison – a turning point in the French Revolution. In the morning crowds gather to watch a military parade along the Champs-Élysées.





Winter Semester

If you wish to visit Paris in Winters,you will definetly not regret it.The City of Lights prove its name on the Christmas Eve and the New Year's Eve. Whole of the city is decorated with blushess and glamour covers the city. The fireworks add a sense of pleasure and the theare,music bands at the effiel tower makes the city roar in happiness.
