Events And Festivals

Though Goa is considered as the most westernized state of India, it celebrates all the events that occur in the western countries of the world but with a difference along with Indian festivals.

The Bregenz Festival

This festival is organized at one of the best settings, the world’s largest lake stage. This lovely setting and the stage design are certainly the highlights of the festival other than the awesome concerts that take place during the festival.

The festival sees international artists, musicians and travelers from all over the globe to perform and enjoy classical concerts. Be a part of this festival this year and have a great time at Lake Constance.






Salzburg Festival

One of the most popular and longest-running Austria summer festivals in , Salzburg Summer Festival would complete 100 years this year. The festival first began in 1920 and today it is famous all around the globe. Completing a century, this year the festival would be greater and if you haven’t booked your tickets already, then you should! Enjoy opera, music, theatre and a lot of other fun activities during this cultural extravaganza!

Vienna Weihnachtsmarkt

If we are talking about Austria festivals, then we cannot leave out Christmas as it is one of the major festivals celebrated in this country. But what is even more exciting, is the festivity of the Christmas market! Weihnachtsmarkt is a Christmas street market which is set up from the mid of November and continues till Christmas.

Bergisel Ski Jump

A lot has changed since the first ski jumpers came racing down the Bergisel in 1927, with a winning distance of 47.5 metres. It is an architectural masterpiece. The hill record, set up by Michael Hayböck in 2015, has almost tripled in distance to 138 metres.
